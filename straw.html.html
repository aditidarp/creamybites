Strawberry

Fresh strawberries are sourced from Mahabaleshwar.They are washed and the leaves are removed from the fruit. The fruit is cut into pieces. They are added to sugar to make the pulp. The strawberry pulp is added to plain rabadi to make the icecream.

INGREDIENTS :

Milk,Sugar,Strawberry Pulp
